# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a respectful, professional community for everyone.

## Expected behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask questions before escalating.
- Keep discussions focused on the work.

## Unacceptable behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Public disclosure of private information.
- Threats or intimidation.

## Enforcement
Report issues to security@custos.local. We may remove content or participants who violate this code.
